"Return to Your Senses" Home Retreat

A holistic introduction to self-care for yourself and your friends or family - a new kind of 'pamper party'.

This is a three hour workshop, held in your own home! Just bring yourself and some comfy clothes!

Healthy snacks will be provided.

Each of the below listed practices aims to increase your self awareness and connect you to the present moment... So often nowadays we are living such busy lives – it has become so normalised and accepted to be stressed in some way, in turn our time is used up thinking about the next thing and often we are 'living in our heads' – in our thoughts and can forget to live in the moment.

This workshop will introduce ways in which to bring simple strategies into your everyday life, as well as sharing a wonderful experience connecting with friends or family in the comfort of your home.

The retreat includes:

  • Mindful movement – this focuses more on the mind body connection than getting fit and offers a different approach to exercise
  • Mindful eating – a selection of foods will be sampled
  • Meditation – a short introduction to meditation and its benefits
  • Massage – for self care and to connect with loved ones using aromatherapy oils

Throughout the retreat we will have 'conscious conversations' - real talk and learn ways to truly listen to one another...

This is a great way to spend an afternoon connecting with friends and stimulating your senses!

"Creative Dreaming" Home Retreat

A creative afternoon for yourself and your friends or family - a new kind of 'pamper party'.

Maybe you're feeling a bit lost or overwhelmed with life, or you've got all of your ducks in a row but still something feels like it's missing? Or perhaps there are things that you really want in life but you can't seem to get at them?

This home retreat is a place to give you and a group of your friends time to consider what possibilities you have to truly lead a life you want.

This is a three hour workshop, held in your own home! Just bring yourself and some comfy clothes!

Healthy snacks will be provided.

The retreat includes:

  • Guided Visualization – be guided in this light meditation to help you visualize what you truly want in your life!
  • Vision boarding – a practical session in where we get creative! Outcome scissor, glue, sparkles if you like, some chilled music and giggles with friends
    • Your vision board could be a theme (work/health/home), what your 'perfect day' looks like or if you're not sure what you want then anything that takes your fancy...
  • Affirmations and manifesting your dreams – learn tools to make it all happen!
