Transformational Coaching

To help you on your journey of self-discovery and real change I have put together three coaching packages that lay out the foundations to achieving your personal goals and intentions.

These packages are just an outline – each package can be tailored to your needs AND your timeframe, if you just want coaching without the extras of course this can also be done!

What I love the most is finding that spark and building a trusting relationship with my clients, I have spent many years working one to one and facilitating groups, I provide a safe, open, and supportive coaching space for possibilities to come into fruition!

Who are my clients?

  • Busy 'stressed' working people
  • Health care professionals
  • Corporate professionals
  • Clients with an existing health care condition*
  • Clients wishing to prevent health care conditions*
  • Creatives and entrepreneurs

*Please note I am a qualified Occupational Therapist skilled in working with behaviour change, disease self-management and stress management.

What will you gain from working with me?!

  • Reduced stress levels
  • Clarity
  • A tool box of health and wellness strategies
  • Renewed energy and lust for life
  • Numerous possibilities
  • Self-efficacy: the belief that you can make a change!
  • Momentum!

"Let's Do This!" Coaching Package

It takes 66 days to change a habit. Take things at a steady pace and see the transformation that takes place.

Designed to get you going, "Let's Do This!" includes six coaching sessions over a 10 week period (though this can be adjusted to suit your individual preferences).

What You Get:

  • Complimentary 30 minute discovery call (via phone or Skype)
  • Holistic assessment to help you identify where you are now and where you want to be
  • 90 minute Intention Setting Coaching Session
  • Five 1 hour coaching sessions (planned fortnightly)
  • Follow up notes from each session together
  • Worksheet templates to keep you on track
  • 10 Call Boosters (approximately 10 minutes each, once a week on average)
  • Email support throughout the entire 10 weeks
