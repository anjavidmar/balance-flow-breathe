About Me

Hi I'm Louise, I am a transformational wellbeing coach, mindfulness facilitator and registered Occupational Therapist with a career focused on self-management, stress-management, behaviour change and confidence building.

During my career I've seen first-hand the impact that a lack of purpose and meaning in life has on the quality of people's lives and have made it my mission to make sure people like you shift that 'stuckness' into a thriving life!

Having struggled personally with stress and overwhelm, a lack of confidence and feeling lost and not myself, my experience has led me to believe, to know, that there is another way to do life, it feels easier and is a much freer way of living — I promise!

So, if you're ready for change and a lasting improvement to your health, wellbeing and daily life — contact me for a complementary, and obligation free, 30 minute discovery session (also known as a friendly chat!) and we can see if we are a good fit to work together!

Yes please, I'd like to know more!
